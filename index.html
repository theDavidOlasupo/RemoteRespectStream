Claudette Cameron (May 21, 1938 - May 6, 2021) Claudette was predeceased by her husband and best friend Raymond. Survived by her six loving daughters, Laurianne (Brian) Evans, Marlene Othmer (Marc LeBeau), •Nancy Othmer (Shane Barclay), Patricia Othmer (Carlo Matulich), Phemie (Denis) LaRue and Cindy Ellis. Grams or GMA to 9 grandchildren, whose lives she played an integral part in; Raymond, Justin, Amanda, Mitchell, Sara, Alexandria, Tyler, Madison and MacKenzie. She was GGMA to 12 great-grand-children. Daughter of the late Gerard Chenier & Violet Ethel (née Smith). Beloved sister to Ronald (Maureen), Richard (Claire), and Normand Chenier (late Fran).

My Moment with God: Dear God, you have given me another gift another day of life. Help me to remember this as the day unfolds. If today is shadowed by sadness or heartache give me the strength and courage to hold my head high and keep my faith strong. If today is bright with joy and happiness, help me to treasure each moment. Remind me today to say "I love you" to those I love, because tomorrow might be too late, Let me find the time today to let someone know I am thinking of him or her make me a bit kinder and more thoughtful than I was yesterday. And, dear Lord please make the actions of my heart speak louder than the sounds of my tongue, Amen.
